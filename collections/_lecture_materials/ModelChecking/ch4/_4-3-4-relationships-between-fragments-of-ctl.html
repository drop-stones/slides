Five logics have different expressive powers.
  • CTL*: most powerful
    • OK: \( \bm{AFG} p, \bm{AG}(\bm{EF}p), \bm{AFG}p \lor \bm{AG}(\bm{EF}p) \)
  • CTL: path quantifier + temporal operator
    • OK: \( \bm{AG}(\bm{EF}p) \)
    • NG: \( \bm{AG}(\bm{EF}p), \bm{AFG}p \lor \bm{AG}(\bm{EF}p)\)
  • LTL: \( \bm{A} f \)
    • OK: \( \bm{AFG} p \)
    • NG: \( \bm{AG}(\bm{EF}p), \bm{AFG}p \lor \bm{AG}(\bm{EF}p) \)
